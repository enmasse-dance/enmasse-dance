Our home, a 140 acre permaculture farm in the foothills of the Welsh Black Mountains, will be transformed into an immersive otherworld centred on carefully curated & produced dancefloors. The artists entrusted with your musical trip will only be revealed closer to the time.

The site comes fully equipped. There will be eco-friendly camping facilities, furnished bell tents, coaches from Bristol, delicious food vendors, artistic installations, as well as sheltered dance floors with a late licence for maximal merriment until dawn.

We aim to bring together a diverse party crowd, with a mutual respect for each other and our surroundings. To preserve the intimacy of the dance, capacity is strictly limited.

Friends old, new & soon to be, thank you for trusting us with your hard earned weekend. We look forward to welcoming you En Masse and encourage you to take the Friday off so you can pitch and relax ahead of our maiden musical voyage together. See you on the 12th June 2020!
